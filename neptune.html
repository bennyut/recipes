“I can endure anything as long as I'm with you, even if we are being burned by the fires of hell.”

Michiru Kaiou is a prodigious student of Mugen Academy. Stunningly beautiful, unrelentingly kind, and incredibly intelligent, Michiru is the absolute perfect example of a fairy-tale princess. While she does happen to be famous in the music world for being an a phenomenal violin player, her biggest feat might just be helping Sailor Moon and her friends defeat some of the greatest evils that have ever been known to mankind. Don't let her feminine elegance fool you, she is one of the most powerful warriors the universe has ever known.
