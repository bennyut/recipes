"Protected by Saturn, the Outer Planet of Destruction, the Guardian of Silence. I am Sailor Saturn."

Hotaru Tomoe may appear to be your average 12-year-old, but don't be fooled! Despite her kind appearance, she is one of the most powerful protectors of the cosmos! With her deadly Silence Glaive in hand, she is capable of destroying entire planets with just one swing. While she has been tempted by evil in the past, rest assured, she is and will always be on the side of good.

    Notable attacks:

  • Silence Wall
  • Silence Glaive Surprise
  • Death Reborn Revolution

    Instructions on making your own Sailor Saturn:

  1. Be the father to a badly injured child.
  2. Replace parts of her limbs with machinery.
  3. Implant a Daimon, Mistress 9 inside your child.
  4. Congrats! Several years later, she will awaken as Sailor Saturn. Enjoy your Guardian of Silence!
